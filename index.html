    Dr. Uttam K. Bhui is currently Associate Professor at School of Petroleum
    Technology,Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU, Formerly Pandit
    Deendayal Petroleum University-PDPU), Gandhinagar,Gujarat. He ob
    -tained M. Sc. (Applied Geology – Gold Medalist)) and Ph. D. (Sc.) degree
    from Jadavpur University,Kolkata. Dr. Bhui has both industrial (ONGC)
    and academic (Jadavpur University, PDPU) experience. He worked in
    ONGC as Reservoir Engineer (1999-2002) and was involved in reservoir
    operation and field development activities for reservoir management. Dr.
    Bhui during his stay with Jadavpur University as Research Scholar (1993-
    99) and as a faculty member (2002-2008) in the Dept. of Geological Sci
    -ences, developed his expertise in geological mapping and geochemical da
    -ta (phase,bulk,chemical and stable isotope) handling for magmatic rocks
    and associated economic mineral deposits particularly chromium, vanadi
    -um, nickel and platinum group of elements. Dr. Bhui, in his present aca
    -demic position at PDEU, involved in teaching related to Petroleum Geo
    -science and Reservoir Engineering courses for Petroleum Engineering
    programs in both undergraduate and post-graduate level. Keeping energy
    security and sustainability of India in mind, Dr. Bhui increased his re
    - search horizon aligning clean energy and green environment of mother
    Earth for sustainable development of human civilization. His present re
    -search activities are related to characterization of hydrocarbon components
    in molecular level from crude oil, coal and carbonaceous shale and their
    application in exploration, exploitation and utilization to meet the futuristic
    need of energy and chemicals for sustainable development. For greener
    environment he is constantly putting his efforts for Geological sequestra
    -tion of CO2 and Geological disposal of nuclear wastes.
